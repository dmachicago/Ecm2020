About Contacts Form Page

The contacts form allows you to have a quick archive for your contacts.  Please note that currently this screen has limited functions and support. 

First archive: To archive your contacts for the first time, simply click on archive contacts.  It will then process all the contacts in your Outlook Contacts and populate the grid the information you have on each contact.

Update archives: To update your archive later you will need to manually archive these contacts each time.   To”overwrite (the archive into the repository) if exists”, please select the button at the bottom of the Archive Contacts Screen,  then click on refresh on the upper left hand of the window.  While the screen is open, archive contacts again and it will overwrite your information.

Restore allows you to restore an archived contact into your Outlook contacts or overwrite it in your Outlooks Contact if it already exists.
